Issue: "Outlook cannot connect to server"
- Check network connectivity.
- Restart Outlook.
- Reset Outlook profile via Control Panel → Mail → Profiles.
- Validate modern authentication is enabled.
- Confirm no stale password stored in Credential Manager.
- Check service health on https://status.office.com.
Issue: Email stuck in Outbox
- Switch Outlook to Online Mode.
- Ensure the mailbox is not over quota.
- Disable large attachment uploads via VPN.
Issue: Missing Emails
- Verify filters & rules.
- Check Junk and Clutter folders.
- Confirm retention policy has not auto-archived items.
Issue: Cannot send attachments
- Max attachment size: 25 MB.
- For larger files, use OneDrive → Share Link.
