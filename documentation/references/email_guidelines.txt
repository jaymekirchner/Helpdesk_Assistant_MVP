Overview:
This document outlines email security requirements for all employees.
Security Controls:
- MFA required for all email access.
- External email tagging applied to all inbound messages.
- Advanced Threat Protection (ATP) scans attachments & links.
- DKIM, DMARC, SPF enforced for outbound domains.
Phishing Handling:
1. If user reports phishing:
   - Send email sample to phishing@company.internal.
   - Security team analyzes via ATP.
2. Educate user on safe handling.
3. If compromised inbox detected:
   - Force password reset.
   - Revoke active sessions.
   - Run mailbox auditing script.
Encryption:
Confidential data must be sent using:
- “Encrypt Message” in Outlook
- Or M365 Message Encryption (OME)

Retention:
- Standard retention: 7 years for all employee mailboxes.
- Deleted items retained 30 days unless purged.
Archiving:
- Online Archive mailbox enabled after 45 GB usage.
- Archive mailbox capacity: unlimited.
Legal Hold:
1. Only Legal and Compliance may request holds.
2. Place mailbox on litigation hold via Exchange Online.
3. Hold prevents deletion or modification of emails.
4. Duration determined case-by-case.
Auto-Archive Settings:
- Moves emails older than 2 years to Archive.
