Requirements:
- Minimum length: 12 characters
- Must contain upper/lower/number/special
- Cannot match last 10 passwords
- Expires every 90 days
Best Practices:
- Use passphrases (e.g., 'Sunset-River-Cloud9')
- Avoid personal info (birthdays, pets, addresses)
- Never reuse passwords between systems
- Enable password manager integration
After Reset:
- Update all signed-in devices:
   - Outlook (desktop/mobile)
   - VPN client
   - Teams/OneDrive
- Clear cached credentials to avoid lockouts.
Locked Account After Reset?
- Device may be retrying old credentials.
- Remove credentials from Windows Credential Manager.
